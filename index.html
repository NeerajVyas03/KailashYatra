Kailash Mansarovar Yatra, June 2018.

Day 1: Departure from Jodhpur

Our journey to Kailash started on the evening of June 24th. All of our friends and family gathered @Jodhpur railway station to wish us well for our journey ahead. We also visited our Guru sthan and met our elders in the morning

View Pictures
Day 2-4: ITBP, Delhi

We spent the next two days in Delhi where we completed the visa formalities at the Ministry of External Affairs and got our medical certificate at DHLI.
We were also briefed about the Yatra ahead along with our batchmates.

View Pictures
Day 5: Beginning the Yatra

Our Yatra officially started on the morning of June, 28th where we, along with all the members of batch-5, KMY-2018 proceeded towards our next halt.
We were wished well by our relatives and fellow Kailashis at Gujarati Sadan, Delhi.

View Pictures
Day 6: Meeting the Yogi

On the morning of June, 29th we arrived at KMVN Guest House, Dania and proceeded towards Almora, where we interacted with the mystic yogi, Baba Kailash Nath Ji, and got his blessings for the first time.
We also visited ancient Gollu Devta Temple, on our way to Almora.

View Pictures
Day 7-12: Arrival in village Gunji

Gunji marks the beginning of our trek to Kailash. We stayed in the village to acclimatize our body.
We had our medical check-up by military doctors posted in the village.

View Pictures
Day 13-14: Trek towards Om Parvat

We began our trek to Om parvat on July 6th and visited the Kali Mata Mandir on our way to Om Parvat.
We were lucky enough the witness the beauty of Om Parvrat all day long as we took halt at the base camp near Om Parvat.

View Pictures
Day 15-16: Anniversary Celebration in China

On July 9th, we celebrated our Anniversaryin China after crossing the border on the morningof 8th July, along with all of our batchmates.



View Pictures
Day 17: Lake Mansarovar

The next morning we visited the serene Lake Mansarovar and took a dip in the holy water of the Lake.
We also saw the north face of Mt. Kailash for the first time, across Lake Mansarovar.

View Pictures
Day 18-21: Kailash Parikrama & Charan Poojan

We began our parikrama around Mt. Kailash on the morning of July 11th and performed Charan Poojan at the nearest point to Mt. Kailash and continued our trek to Dolma Pass and Gauri Kund.

View Pictures
Day 22-32: Journey, back home

After completing our parikrama we began our journey back to where we came from on the morning of 15th July. We reached Delhi on 26th July and reached our hometown Jodhpur the following day.

View Pictures
Day 33: Grand Welcome in Jodhpur

We reached Jodhpur on 27th July and recieved a very grand welcome by our family at Jodhpur railway station for the completion of our Kailash Yatra.
All of our family gathered at our parental house to greet us and to hear stories about the mystical Mt. Kailash.
